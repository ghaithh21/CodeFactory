La liga

Zinedine Zidane is a French football legend who has won the La Liga title three times with Real Madrid as a manager, in 2016-2017, 2017-2018, and 2019-2020. He is also a former player who won the La Liga title with Real Madrid in 2002 and the UEFA Champions League with Real Madrid in 2002, and was named the FIFA World Player of the Year in 1998.
